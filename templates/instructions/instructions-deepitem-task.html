DEEPITEM-TASK You will be asked to make a judgment about each word in the list. Specifically, you will decide whether or not the word refers to an object that could fit into a regular shoebox.

There are two possible choices for each word: "Yes" and "No". You will respond by pressing the "y" key for "Yes" or the "n" key for "No".

For example, if we asked you to judge the word "baseball", you would press "y" because a baseball can certainly fit into a shoebox.

For some words, it won't be clear what the right answer is; just answer according to your first reaction to the word.

The task will automatically advance to the next word after a fixed amount of time. There will be a brief screen showing a cross "+" between each word presentation.
