Call for participants

The Cognitive Variability Laboratory at Michigan State University is looking for participants in a research study of cognitive processing.

During this study you will view, words, letters, pictures, or digits on a computer screen. You may be asked to make simple judgments about these items (e.g., “would this item fit in a shoebox?”).

You will also complete some reasoning tasks that involve solving problems. These problems involve patterns composed of words, numbers, or pictures. You will try to determine which item completes the pattern, what rule the pattern follows, or what items break the rule.

Before each task you will receive detailed instructions and be given a chance to practice. The tasks are designed to be moderately difficult; it is normal to find the tasks challenging.

Because some of the material involves English words, you must be a native English speaker to participate in this research.

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in this research.
